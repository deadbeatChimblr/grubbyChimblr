RWBY House was a Khonjin House parody that I mainly wrote after IceCreamJaxxie sent me an audio recording of her attempt of a cartoony voice, that of which I wanted to animate. At the time, Khonjin had just releaed the "MAKE YOUR OWN KHONJIN" .fla file, which included all the original Khonjin assets, although I was also working on my own at the time that traced over the assets (such as Geno, who turned out to be a bitmap in the actual .fla).

A few episodes of RWBY House were written, with 3 episodes total. A fourth and a intro were done, but only the intro remains and episode 4 was never animated. Spencer actually voices some of the characters. We used to be friends, then rivals, now we kinda just look at each other funny. With that in mind, I don't think continuing RWBY House is a worthwhile idea, and I'm not even that interested in RWBY. Neo's not supposed to speak, but low and behold, here she is voiced by Spencer of all people.

I'd get into more on RWBY House's eventual cancellation, but the details are pretty iffy, and it's best to move on.
