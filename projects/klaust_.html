Klaust_ is a Yume Nikki fan-game about a girl named Rikotsura, who receives a prototype for a headset capable of granting users the ability to explore their dreams while conscious, and manipulate them through thoughts alone.

Although exploring your subconscious and reliving memories might seem like a good idea at first, perhaps there are some things which are best left forgotten... Or at least things you'd rather not think about.

The game is currently being worked on (albeit slowly), and there are no plans to announce a release date as of now.

Any related wallpapers to the game are available under the wallpapers download linked at the downloads page.
